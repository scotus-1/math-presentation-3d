question 1:

Suppose the screen is represented by a rectangle in the yz-plane with vertices (0,±400, 0) and (0,±400,600), and the camera is placed at (1000,0,0). A line L in the scene passes through the points (230,-285,102) and (860,105,264).

At what points should L be clipped by the clipping planes?
